Our Team

We are students from Nichols school. We each have a passion for robotics and engineering. Ever since 2013, our team has worked hard to create robots to compete in the FIRST Robotics Challenge. Since then, we have also started a FLL team and FTC team for our school, along with multiple teams in other schools.
